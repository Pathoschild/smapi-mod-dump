Chest Manager


The Chest Manager allows you to drag-and-drop items between Chests, your Backpack and the Shipping Bin.

Select the source and destinations and you can then drag-and-drop items between the 2 chests.

Move your cursor over the Item you wish to grab and the browser pointer will change to the move cursor when you are over the Item image.

You can then drag the Item to the other selected Chest, the Garbage Bin or the Shipping Bin.

Pressing the 'Refresh' button in either of the Chest boxes will reload the Chest contents, in case you have made changes to the Chest in the Game.

Stardew Web reads the Category and Name settings of the Chest when you are using the mod Chests Anywhere.

I have tested Chest Manager with:
  • Chests
  • Backpacks
  • Shipping Bin
  • Farm House Fridge
  • Mini-Fridge
  • Junimo Huts
  • Auto-Grabbers
  • Fish Tanks
  • Dressers
  • Overworld Chests - sometimes throws a non-fatal error
Currently known not supported:
  • Item Bags (Coming soon.)
