Deinstallation Guide

======================================================

Under some circumstances your savegame might be affected by
the changes of this bundle mod. To revert the changes follow
the following guideline:

* Set the ChallengingCommuntyCenter config to "Pure_Vanilla"
  * The config can be found in the ChallengingCommuntyCenter folder
* Load your affected savegames and play at least one day till the game
  saved the changes
  * This will reset all bundle entries in the savegame to the pure
    vanilla counterparts
* After that please delete all reminders of this mod
