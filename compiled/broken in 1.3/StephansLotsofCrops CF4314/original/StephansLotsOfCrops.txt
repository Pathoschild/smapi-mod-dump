Stephan's Lots of Crops 1.4

This mod adds 9 new crops to Stardew Valley. Not compatible with any other mod that modifies the same XNB files.

-Special Thanks-
MysticTempest for updating my mod in my absence as a part of the compatibility version with MysticTempest's Tea mod.

-Installation-

1. Backup your Content Folder of Stardew Valley.

2. Extract the folders into the Stardew Valley directory.
