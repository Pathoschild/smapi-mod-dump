[size=4][b][font=Verdana]How do I get it?[/font][/b][/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/vzNOzyj.png[/img][size=3][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]The Propagator isn't immediately available – by default you'll need [b][color=#efefef]a cellar in the farmhouse, and mushrooms in the cave.[/color][/b]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Demetrius will come visit you the morning after Robin finishes work on your house to deliver the recipe.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]This mod is officially sponsored by the 'didnt-pick-fruit-cave' gang.


[font=Verdana][b][size=4]How does it work?[/size][/b][/font]

[size=3][b][img]https://i.imgur.com/hBXucjp.png[/img]Crafting[/b][/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]The propagation log is craftable for the same ingredients as a cask – [color=#efefef][b]20 wood, 1 hardwood[/b][/color]. Drop a mushroom onto the log to start growing!

[size=3][b][img]https://i.imgur.com/hBXucjp.png[/img]Growing[/b][/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]At the end of the day, each of the logs will grow a little more.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]A common mushroom will typically grow every night, a red mushroom every two nights, and a purple mushroom every four nights.
   ﻿Custom mushrooms added with Json Assets, like Shiitake Mushrooms and Black Fungus, will grow relative to their value.
   You can leave the logs for a few days and they'll keep growing more mushrooms for their stacks for when you check on them.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]The logs can only hold so many of each type of mushroom, though: the bigger and better mushrooms will grow smaller stacks.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Keep an eye out for logs with huge, fully-grown mushrooms: they've reached their capacity, and you'll need to pop them off to keep growing.

[size=3][b][img]https://i.imgur.com/hBXucjp.png[/img]Interacting[/b][/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Click while holding a mushroom to set it as the root-mushroom for an empty log. If the log's already got one, it'll pop off and swap to yours.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Click the log later on to pop any extra mushrooms off it, and click it again to pop the root-mushroom off.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Like the Casks in your cellar, you can whack them with an axe or a hoe to pop off the root-mushroom, too.

[size=3][b][img]https://i.imgur.com/hBXucjp.png[/img]Professions[/b][/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Your [b]Foraging[/b] professions improve the mushrooms you grow!
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]With the [color=#efefef][b]Gatherer[/b][/color] profession, you'll get a chance for a bonus mushroom on each harvest.
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]Once you've reached the [b]Botanist[/b] profession, you'll pluck only the highest-quality mushrooms.


[b][size=4][font=Verdana]How to configure[/font]?[/size][/b]
[size=3][font=Courier New][font=Trebuchet MS]launching stardew creates a [/font][/font][color=#b6d7a8][b][font=Courier New]config.json[/font][/b][/color][font=Courier New][font=Trebuchet MS], just edit it and re-launch[/font][/font][font=Courier New][font=Trebuchet MS]![/font][/font][/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3]If you'd like to use the logs in places other than your cellar and the cave, you can enable them almost anywhere else, too.[/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]DisabledForFruitCave[/font][/b][/color] – Picked the fruit cave and you still want to grow more mushrooms? I'll allow it![/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]RecipeAlwaysAvailable[/font][/b][/color] – No time for Demetrius? Don't want a big house? You can get the recipe from day 1![/size]
﻿
[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]MaximumDaysToMature[/font][/b][/color] – Want mushrooms to grow faster or slower to match your other mods? Change the timescale![/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]MaximumQuantityLimitsDoubled[/font][/b][/color] – Don't check the cellar often? The total mushrooms each log holds can be raised![/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]OnlyToolsCanRemoveRootMushrooms[/font][/b][/color] – Want better control over your logs? Clicking won't pop mushrooms off using this![/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]PulseWhenGrowing[/font][/b][/color] – Don't want the default machine working effect? You don't need it! Set this to false![/size]

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3][color=#f9cb9c][b][font=Courier New]OtherObjectsThatCanBeGrown[/font][/b][/color] – The mushroom machine accepts all custom items named as a "mushroom" or "fungus".[/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]If you have any custom objects that use a different name, like "Red Cap" instead of "Red Cap Mushroom", add them here!

[img]https://i.imgur.com/fIKLTwD.png[/img][size=3]You can't spawn in a working Propagator with CJB Item Spawner.[/size]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]If you're a cheater, set[b][font=Courier New][size=4][color=#f9cb9c] DebugMode [/color][/size][/font][/b]to[b][font=Courier New][size=4][color=#b6d7a8] true [/color][/size][/font][/b]to allow hotkey spawning:
[img]https://i.imgur.com/x8XztE0.png[/img]You can change the key to add one to your inventory, the default's[font=Courier New][size=4][b][color=#b6d7a8] comma (,)[/color][/b][/size][/font].
